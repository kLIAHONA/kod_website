About Me

My name is Kaylie Osmun and I am 22 years old. I graduated with an Associates Degree in Graphic Design in Spring of 2025. I grew up in Washington state and Alaska (no, I did not live in an igloo or have a pet polar bear. I wish I was that cool). I met my-now husband in May of 2023. We dated for nine months then got married in February of 2024. For Christmas of 2025 we got a doberman puppy and named her Athena. Since getting married we have lived in Washington, Ohio, and are getting ready to move to Idaho.

My Design Journey

I began my design journey in grade 10 when I began to paint quotes. I was a broke high school student saving up for college, so I painted on cardboard. I experimented with alignment, fonts, and colors and created quite the stack of paintings. I also got a pair of white tennis shoes that I would paint more intricate things on. In grade 12 I was asked to be the social media manager for a non-profit in my area. My job included creating digital flyers to promote the weekly activities they hosted as well as dates and times for classes and larger activities.

Fast forward a couple years, it's now summer of 2023 and I'm in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada doing volunteer service work. One of my assignments was tobe on a team of eight people who created graphic ads and videos promoting the lessons we taught. I was only able to be on the team for five weeks before I was moved to a small town south of Edmonton to teach. During my time on that design team I created a promotional video and over a dozen graphic ads. I really enjoyed creating these things and that's when I realized I would love to do this as a career. When I got home that winter I began looking into college courses to get a degree in graphic design. I fast-tracked my degree and finished it in just over a year, and the rest is history!
